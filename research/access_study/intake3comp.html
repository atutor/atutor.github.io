The Courseware Accessibility Study

Distance Education Survey Results

The attitudes towards computers were favourable on average (scale score 63, SD=23) ranging from very favourable (31 with 30 being the completely favourable) to  neutral (94 out of a possible 180 for very unfavourable). The responses to each item below are enclosed in brackets [ ] and it should be noted that the odd numbered items were reversed when they were combined into the scale score for attitude toward computers. There were two cases missing from the data set due to unknown reasons.
